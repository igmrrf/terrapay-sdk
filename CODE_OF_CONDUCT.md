# 🤝 Code of Conduct

We want to keep this community a safe, welcoming, and awesome place for everyone. Basically: **be a good human.**

## 🌟 Our Standards

We're all here to learn and build cool stuff together. To keep the vibes high:
- **Be kind and respectful**: We're all human beings on the other side of the screen.
- **Be inclusive**: Everyone is welcome here, regardless of background or experience level.
- **Give constructive feedback**: If you see something that could be better, share it nicely!
- **Help each other out**: If you see someone stuck, lend a hand if you can.

## 🚫 What's NOT okay

We have zero tolerance for:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting, or personal attacks.
- Disrespectful behavior.

If you see something that makes you uncomfortable, please reach out to us at [francis.igbiriki@gmail.com]. We'll handle it privately and fairly.

Let's keep the focus on building great things and having fun! ✨
